Classes and Specs

Classes

Death Knight

Death Knights are undead warriors formerly in the service of the Lich King. These brutal warriors use the power of necromancy, shadow and frost magic, and brute strength to dominate their foes. Death Knights are a plate wearing class and can take the role of a tank or melee DPS. Specs are Blood for tanking, Frost melee DPS, and Unholy for melee DPS. Blood focuses on self healing to survive encounters, Frost specializes in dual wielding weapons and using frost magic, and Unholy is focused on summoning undead and using diseases on the opponent.

Demon Hunter

Demon Hunters are warriors personally chosen by Illadin Stormrage to combat the Burning Legion. They are elves that have had their eyesight removed and infused with fel energies. They are a ferocious and agile fighter that specializes in decimating demonic threats. They are a leather wearing class and can take the role of tank or melee DPS. Specs are Havoc for melee DPS and Vengeance for tanking. Havoc is a very fast and mobile spec focusing on eye beams and transforming in a demonic entity. Vengeance utilizes flames, spikes, and demonic transformations to mitigate damage.

Druid

Druids are shapeshifters that devote themselves to the reverence of nature. They utilize the powers of nature to protect and heal others by shapeshifting into various animals such as bears, panthers, hawks, and the mystical Moonkin. They are a versatile class that can fill any role in group content. They are a leather wearing class that can take the role of tank, healer, melee DPS, and ranged DPS. Specs are Balance for ranged DPS, Feral for melee DPS, Guardian for tank, and Restoration for healing. Balance uses the Moonkin form to harness nature and arcane spells to defeat opponents. Feral focuses around transforming into panther and is a fast class that uses bleeding damage and combos to deal with enemies. Guardian focuses on the bear form to mitigate damage and thrash opponents. Restoration harnesses the healing attributes of nature to revitalize and cleans allies, their special form is the healing treant.

Evoker

Evokers are an elite class of Dracthyr, a humanoid dragon race, created by the earth aspect and leader of the Black Dragonflight Neltharion. These warriors harness the powers of the 5 dragon flights to protect the Dragon Isles and the secrets Neltharion has kept hidden there. They are a mail wearing class that can take the role of ranged DPS and healer. Specs are Augmentation for ranged DPS/support, Devastation for ranged DPS, and Preservation for healing. Augmentation uses powers of the black and bronze dragon flights to damage foes and bolster allies. Devastation uses the powers of the blue dragon flight to harness the destructive powers of the arcane. Preservation harnesses the powers of the green and red dragon flights to heal and preserve life.

Hunter

Hunters are rangers and beast masters of the World of Warcraft. They specialize in taming beasts, survival and wilderness skills, and are adept in many weapons. The ferocious beasts they tame can serve as powerful allies to help support the hunter in any combat scenario. They are a mail wearing class that are pure DPS. Specs are Beast Mastery for ranged DPS, Marksmen for ranged DPS, and Survival for melee DPS. Beast Mastery focuses on using tamed beasts to dish out damage. Marksmen is less focused on pets and is more about archery or guns. Survival focuses on 2 handed melee weapons and traps.

Mage

Mages are masters of all aspects of the arcane. From conjuring devastating elemental storms, portals and teleportation, creating food and water, to manipulating time itself. They are a cloth wearing class that is pure ranged DPS. Specs are Arcane, Fire, and Frost for ranged DPS. Arcane focuses on temporal manipulation and spending your mana to increase damage output. Fire is utilizing powerful fire spells and a strong focus on critical strike chance for massive burst damage. Frost utilizes frost spells to freeze and slow enemies and summoning water elementals.

Paladin

Paladins are devout followers of the light and ardent defenders against all things considered unholy who also train heavily in armed and martial combat. They are relentless in their crusade against evil and dogmatic in their faith. They can provide blessings to benefit all allies in their group. They are a plate wearing class that can take the roles of tank, melee DPS, and healer. Specs are Holy for healing, Protection for tanking, and Retribution for melee DPS. Holy uses their unyielding faith to call the light to heal and protect their allies in battle. Protection uses their faith to stand firm against any threats and shield themselves and their allies from dangers. Retribution is the holy crusader that specializes in using 2 handed weapons to smite their foes with holy wrath.

Priest

Priests are similar to paladins with the key difference that they aren't trained in armed combat and utilize more than just the light. Priests walk a path of balance and can call upon both the light and shadows to heal their allies or smite their foes. They are a cloth wearing class that can take the roles of healer and ranged DPS. Specs are Discipline for healer, Holy for healer, and Shadow for ranged DPS. Discipline priests are healing and shielding class that harnesses both light and shadow to protect their allies. They offer a unique play style with their Atonement ability which heals their allies as they damage their foes. Holy priests are healers that utilize the power of the light to heal mass groups of allies. Shadow priests are priests that utilize the powers of shadow and the void to dominate their foes in combat. They can afflict the minds of their opponents and slowly drain their life. Shadow is also my all time favorite spec.

Rogue

Rogues are the assassins of Warcraft. They strike from the shadows with precise and deadly strikes. They utilize methods that many consider unfair and dishonorable like poisons, blinding powder, and deception to defeat their foes. They are a leather wearing class that is purely melee DPS. Specs are Assassination, Outlaw, and Subtlety. Assassination focuses on poisons and bleeding effects to defeat their enemies, Outlaw is very similar to swashbuckler, and Subtlety is focused on ambushing foes from the shadows with quick and deadly precision then retreating back to the shadows.

Shaman

Shamans are similar to druids in that they are nature focused but shamans are more in line with the elements, drawing directly from their powers. They use totems to provide assistance to their allies in battle. They are a mail wearing class that can take the roles of melee DPS, ranged DPS, and healer. Specs are Elemental for ranged DPS, Enhancement for melee DPS, and Restoration for healing. Elemental focuses on channeling the elements into raw damage such as hurling bolts of lava, summoning earthquakes, and freezing enemies with frost shocks. Enhancement focuses on using the elements to enhance the shaman's weapon. Restoration calls upon the healing powers of the elements to heal and revitalize their allies. From healing rains, healing waves, and wind totems.

Warlock

Warlocks are summoners and cultists that summon demons from the Twisting Nether to do their bidding. They make pacts with these demons to cause fear into the hearts of their enemies using their dark and twisted demonic powers. They are a cloth wearing spec that is purely ranged DPS. Specs are Affliction, Demonology, and Destruction. Affliction uses the powers of curses and sickness to slowly drain their opponent's life. Demonology focuses on summoning powerful demons like Dreadtalkers, Imps, Succubus, Felguards, and Infernals to do their bidding. Destruction focuses on the powers of shadow, fire, and chaos magic to obliterate their foes

Warrior

Warriors are the embodiment of strength in Warcraft. They use their brute force and tenacity to dominate their foes in brutal combat. They are a plate wearing class that can take the role of tank and melee DPS. Specs are Arms and Fury for melee DPS, and Protection for tanking. Arms focuses on using a massive 2 handed weapon to smack their foes hard. Fury are the berserkers or barbarians of Warcraft. They dual wield 2 handed weapons and enter states of rage and bloodlust to decimate their foes. Protection is the traditional sword and board style of tank. They block damage and hit hard.
