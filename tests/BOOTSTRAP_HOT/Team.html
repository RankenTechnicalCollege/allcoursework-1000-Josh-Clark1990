Nina Purpleton

Lead Engineer

Nina is a lead engineer in the Mobile Suit Division of Anaheim Electronics. She has worked directly in the Gundam Development Project and played a major role in the development of GP01 and GP02 RX-78 variants. Nina was heavily involved in the events of Operation Stardust, which involved the Zeon forces infiltrating an Earth Federation Base on Earth and stealing GP02, and worked with the Earth Federation Forces to recover the stolen mobile suit with nuclear potential.

Wong Lee

Executive and Primary Investor

Wong Lee is a primary investor in Anaheim Electronics and played a major role in the funding of the Anti Earth Unification Group, also known as the AUEG. He plays a major role in funding the conflict between the AUEG and the branch of the Earth Federation Forces known as the Titans under the command of Captain Bask.

Lucette Audevive

Systems Engineer

Lucette Audevie was an Anaheim Electronics employee involved with the Gundam Development Project. She was a systems engineer assigned to work on the RX-78GP03 Gundam "Dendrobium" for development and testing, alongside Defrah Kar. She helped Kou Uraki steal Gundam Unit 3 from the La Vie en Rose, but was shot and killed in the process by Federation officer Nakohha Nakato.

O'Sulivan

Head of Van Braun Office

O'Sullivan was in charge of the office and headquarters on the moon in the city of Van Bruan. While he openly supported the Earth Federation he was more than happy to make deals with Zeon, including Lieutenant Command Cima Garahau, who played a major role in Operation Stardust. Following the outcome of Operation Stardust, O'Sullivan was found dead in his office, having apparently shot himself in the head rather than be arrested for his implications in the Zeon uprising. However, he had a horrified look on his face when he died, implying that his death may have been a murder made to look like a suicide.
