Smoked Citrus Pork Tacos

    Ingredients
  • 5 pound bone in pork shoulder
  • half gallon of orange juice
  • 8 small limes, juice and zest
  • 4 lemons, juice and zest
  • 1 bundle of cilantro
  • 2 whole heads of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/3 cup of salt, for brine
  • flour or corn tortillas
  • half a white onion, diced
    • lime wedges and cilantro, for garnish
  • charcoal and wood chips, apple and cherrywood, for smoker
  • cumin, paprika, brown sugar, mustard powder, garlic powder, cayene, black/white pepper for dry rub
  • mixture of organge juice, apple cider vinegar, and honey for basting during smoking process
    Preparation and Cooking
  1. in a large container cover pork shoulder with orange juice, lemon and lime juice and zest, crushed garlic cloves, salt and sugar,cilantro. Marinade overnight in fridge
  2. next day prepare your smoker, remove pork from brine and pad dry and apply dry rub
  3. put pork on smoker, make sure the heat source is indirect, aiming for 215 to 220.
  4. smoke the pork shoulder for 10 hours, basting it with mixture every hour. pork should reach internal temp of 190 to 200
  5. remove pork from smoker and let sit covered with foil for 20 to 30 minutes
  6. dice your onion, wedge your limes, and finely chop cilantro.
  7. warm up torillas
  8. pull pork, should be tender enough to fall off the bone
  9. build taco, garnish, serve, and enjoy
