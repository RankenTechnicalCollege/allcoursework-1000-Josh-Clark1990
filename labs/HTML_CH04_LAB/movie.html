Ghost in the Shell

Ghost in the Shell is an animated movie released in 1995 based of the manga of the same name by author Masamune Shirow. The film is a cyberpunk action thriller set in the year 2029 in the futuristic setting of New Port City. The movie follows a cyborg working for the public security sector by the name of Major Motoko Kusanagi. She is assigned to tracking down a hacker by the name of "Puppet Master". The movie's animation style is a mix of both traditional cel animation and the use of CGI animation.

    Voice Actors

  • Akio Otsuka as Bato
  • Iemasa Kayumi as The Puppet Master
  • Koichi Yamadera as Togusa
  • Tamio Oki as Chief Aramaki
